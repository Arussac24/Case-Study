Arts For All

Project Overview

Arts For All is a new New York City based non-profit organization curated with the intention to connect children with socio-economic barriers to several artistic opportunities. Although they had a great organization, their web presence needed a facelift. We set out to bring Arts for All into the year 2022.

Roles

As the Project Manager, it was my job to organize tasks in detailed order, and dictae what each person on my team was responsible for with given deadlines.

Research

In the Research stages of our Design process our team followed qualitative research and design thinking methodologies to compare and contrast the insights we gathered. As a result we completed 8 user interviews based on user's past experiences with non-profit organizations.

Arts For All

Before Arts For All can achieve their goals of gaining more supporters donations, and recognition for their outstanding community involvement, Arts For All needs an informative, aesthetically pleasing, and easier to use website and mobile application
